CBMR Data Analytics

The Data Analytics Platform at the Novo Nordisk Foundation Center for Basic Metabolic Research enables scientists and collaborators at the center to seamlessly integrate multidimensional, high-resolution data from diverse sources in a state-of-the-art computing environment.

For this purpose, the Data Analytics Platform makes available the following resources:

HPC Cluster

The HPC cluster "Esrum" is available to all employees at CBMR, as well as their collaborators.

Click here for documentation about how to gain access to and use the cluster.

Cohort Catalog

The cohort catalog provides an overview of human cohort data managed by Data Analytics.

Click here to access the catalog. The catalog is only accessible to CBMR employees using the UCPH VPN.

Software
