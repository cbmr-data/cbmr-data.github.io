1. Contact#

The Data Analytics Platform (formerly the Phenomics Platform) has an open-door policy and you are always welcome to drop by room 07-8-29 (Unit 8E) at the Maersk Tower, if you need help and/or have questions about the cluster. Our office is located directly across from the 8th floor kitchen.

In addition, you may contact us via email at SUND-CBMR-Esrum. Note that that you must use a UCPH e-mail account when writing to us on this address. Mail from external addresses is currently blocked.

If you are on the CBMR Slack server then you also can find us in the #data-analytics channel.
